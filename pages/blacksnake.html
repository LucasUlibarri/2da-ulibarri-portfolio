BlackSnake (AUST)

BlackSnake (AUST) is a company that manufactures recovery strops for various industries, such as mining, 4WD, military, agricultural use, among others. The objective was to create a platform where they could have their wide range of products and following the aesthetic guidelines intended by the client. Being products with double customisation axis (length and tonnage), the main problem was generated in the shop and how this double variable was going to be possible. Finally I was able to solve it by generating it from their API code and the site's database.
